TRAVIS - Trajectory Analyzer and Visualizer

What is TRAVIS?

TRAVIS is a free tool for analyzing and visualizing trajectories from all kinds of Molecular Dynamics or Monte Carlo simulations. It is intended to be easy to install and easy to use.

See http://www.travis-analyzer.de/
